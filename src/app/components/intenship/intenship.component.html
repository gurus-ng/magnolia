The magnolia
internship/volunteer to work program

Helping students make a keen decision concerning their career path. We seek to boost student confidence.

Why start this internship program?
The Reality

Most often in our part of the world, internships are occupied by tertiary students. With that in mind, we're trying to contribute our own quota to the professional world by preparing the youth adequately by introducing them into the corporate world early enough, before they move on to the tertiary level.

We believe that this would help give them a clear career focus in school and also help them put what they learn in school into practice.

What does it seek to achieve?

This would give students the opportunity to explore while boosting their CV.

Allows students to experience the realities of their chosen careers.

Makes them confident decision makers regarding their careers.

Who are the beneficiaries?

Form 1 to Form 3 Senior high school students who go through our mentorship program and are keen on learning.

Who are the benefactors?

The various organizations who signup to give internship opportunities to students.
