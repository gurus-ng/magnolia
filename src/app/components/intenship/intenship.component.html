Why start this intership program?
The Reality

Most often in our part of the world, internships are occupied by tertiary students. We’re trying to contribute our own quota to the professional world by preparing the youth adequately by introducing them into the world of business early enough, before they move on to the tertiary level.

We believe that this would help give them a clear career focus in school and also help them put what they learn in school into practice.

What does it seek to achieve?

This program seeks to boost student confidence,knowledge of what the real world is and also boost their CV’s

It would also help position the mindsets of the youth properly.

Help them make a keen decision concerning their career path.

Who are the beneficiaries?

Form 1 to Form 3 Senior high school students who go through our mentorship program and are keen on learning.

Who are the benefactors?

Form 1 to Form 3 Senior high school students who go through our mentorship program and are keen on learning.
