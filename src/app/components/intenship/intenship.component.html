Why start this internship program?
The Reality

Most often in our part of the world, internships are occupied by tertiary students. With that in mind, we're trying to contribute our own quota to the professional world by preparing the youth adequately by introducing them into the corporate world early enough, before they move on to the tertiary level.

We believe that this would help give them a clear career focus in school and also help them put what they learn in school into practice.
